2015 Call for Applicants

The Clarissa Mack and Patrick Rezandt Bursary distinct purpose seeks to invest in individuals coming from previously disadvantage backgrounds that have demonstrated the capacity and tenacity to overcome huge socio economic challenges.

Please see the About page for more information, and the Criteria page to see if you are eligible.

The closing date for all applications is 30 November 2015. No late applications will be accepted. Please ensure that all the sections of the application form are fully completed.
