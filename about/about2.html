About the Bursary

A substantial majority of the South African population have been poverty stricken for a long time. This has inhibited the potential for children to access quality education. The Clarissa Mack and Patrick Rezandt Bursary distinct purpose seeks to invest in individuals coming from previously disadvantage backgrounds that have demonstrated the capacity and tenacity to overcome huge socio-economic challenges.

The expectations on successful candidates are high thus prospective candidates are required to prove a desire to make an impact to the community and to rise above all adversity. Their academic performance must portray the firm belief and commitment to be successful. Furthermore the family financial situation should confirm the need for this bursary

This bursary seeks to attract candidates who show extraordinary talents, in need of financial aid to pursue higher education for a better future. It aims to ensure equality by building the future of South Africa with gifted leaders.
