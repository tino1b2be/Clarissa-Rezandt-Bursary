Eligible applicants will have to meet these basic requirements as listed below:

  • Must be from the Western Cape.
  • Must have a valid South African Identity Document.
  • Must apply to study at the University of Cape Town as applications to other institutions of higher education will not be considered.
  • Must be in need of financial assistance.
  • Must show a strong academic record (November Grade 11, June and September, Grade 12 academic results provided).
  • Candidates from a previously disadvantaged background will be given preference
  • Great leadership skills will be advantageous.
